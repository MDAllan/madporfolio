Al-Amal Foundation

The Al-Amal Foundation, established in November 2022, is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering unity, empowerment, and spiritual growth among Muslim women. Created in loving memory of Amal El-Bekai — whose name means “hope” in Arabic — the foundation embodies her legacy by nurturing a strong sense of sisterhood rooted in compassion, faith, and education. Through community events, open discussions, and accessible resources, Al-Amal inspires women to grow personally and collectively, while deepening their love for Islam.

Project Overview

Al Aml Foundation was developed as a brand identity and website project. The focus was on creating a professional and trustworthy image that appeals to both donors and beneficiaries, while keeping the site accessible and informative.

My Responsibilities

  • Designed a new logo that visually represents the mission and values of the Al-Amal Foundation — focusing on unity, hope, and empowerment.
  • Developed a complete brand guideline that includes logo variations, safe zones, clear space rules, color codes, and usage ratios to ensure consistent brand application.
  • Built a fully responsive website that adjusts smoothly across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices, offering a seamless user experience for all visitors.
  • Created a structured homepage and subpages to highlight the foundation’s mission, upcoming events, donation options, and community resources.
  • Ensured the design maintained accessibility, including clear font choices, contrast ratios, and mobile-first layout decisions.
  • Presented the full visual identity — logo, brand guide, and website — in a cohesive style that reflects the foundation’s theme of hope and inclusivity.
