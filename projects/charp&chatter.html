Chirp & Chatter

Chirp & Chatter was born out of a passion to provide a haven for parrots who were once pets but became ownerless. The jungle-like immersion of Chirp & Chatter allows potential adopters and bird-lovers to meet parrots in a habitat designed for them. Bonding over shared interests, community can be built over a cup of coffee while patrons interact with the parrots and learn more about them and their stories.

Project Overview

This project involved creating a brand identity and responsive web presence for Chirp & Chatter. The identity needed to balance the playful, natural environment with the emotional impact of giving rescued parrots a second chance at life. The result was a design that fosters connection, warmth, and purpose.

My Responsibilities

  • Designed the Chirp & Chatter logo, drawing inspiration from nature, jungle environments, and parrot silhouettes to evoke warmth and friendliness.
  • Created a full brand guideline document including logo usage, sizing rules, color palette, and typography.
  • Developed a responsive homepage prototype with a welcoming layout and interactive elements showcasing parrots, café ambiance, and adoption info.
  • Illustrated icons and created content sections for community events, parrot bios, and interactive storytelling spaces within the café concept.

Visual Identity

Research & Design Approach

I started with visual research into parrot sanctuaries, natural jungles, and inclusive community cafés. I aimed to translate the feeling of wonder and companionship into the branding. I explored earthy and tropical tones, rounded fonts, and hand-drawn elements that represent approachability and authenticity.

Final Deliverables

  • Logo and branding files in web and print-ready formats.
  • Comprehensive brand guideline PDF for consistent application.
  • Homepage mockup highlighting the parrot experience, café vibe, and adoption messaging.
  • Supporting illustrations and assets for café menus, signage, and merchandise.
